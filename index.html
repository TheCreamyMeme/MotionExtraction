Motion Extraction:

You can extract motion by duplicating a video and overlaying them, inverting the colors (you can also adjust them to your liking), apply some blending such as opacity or mix-blend-mode, then finally having a time delay. All the pixels that have changed will be highlighted while all the pixels that have stayed the same will be dark. Changing the blending mode, opacity settings, and time difference will all affect the output.
